About

Welcome to Escape the Labyrinth

In this game, you must navigate through a dangerous labyrinth filled with traps and obstacles, all while avoiding the crazy scientists who have captured you. Your goal is to make your way down from the 10th floor to the 1st floor, where you can finally escape the building and win the game. Along the way, you'll need to be quick, agile, and resourceful to overcome the challenges that stand in your way. Good luck, and may the odds be ever in your favor!
